My Story

I was predicted to be an Architect by my nursery teacher.
An Archaeologist by many of my peers at school. On my journey to find my calling, I dipped my toes into consulting, marketing, and even politics.

I went as far afield as China to learn about international business. I took on consulting projects. I taught myself a great deal about the commercial world but a part of me longed for creativity. I had a burning desire to build great things.

For two years, I deliberated in pursuing a career in web development. No other experience was as great as learning to code and building projects.

Simply put, I love to build and co-create web apps.
